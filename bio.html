Anureet Dhaliwal

Fitness Trainer and Operator of A1 Fitness

Hi! My name is Anureet Dhaliwal and I am personal trainer based in the Los Angeles area with a masters in fitness medicine and over 10 years in personal training experience. I have owned my own gym (at the age of 23) and have competed in various triathlons, but my true calling in one on one personal training. Eventually, I want to open a gym that serves as a rehabilitation center and as a place for children with Austism to be able to workout and maintain healthy lives.

My goal orientated lifestyle bleeds into my clients' workouts, as strives for the completion of their own goals. Overall, I want my clients to achieve physical, mental, and spiritual health so that they may live their best lives - free from doubt and self consciousness. Whether you want to meet in person or over Zoom, I will put in the work to make sure you get results.
